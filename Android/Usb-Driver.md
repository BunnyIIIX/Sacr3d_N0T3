## [How to Install Huawei Driver on Windows OS]{#How_to_Install_Huawei_Driver_on_Windows_OS}

1.  Download and Extract the *Huawei USB Drivers.zip* file on your
    computer.
2.  Double-click the *DriverSetup.exe*setup file.
3.  This will start the USB Driver Installation Wizard.
4.  Click *Next*, and Agree on the terms and conditions.
5.  On the next screen, choose the destination folder.
6.  At last, press the *Install* button to begin the actual process.
7.  Within a few minutes, a successful message will show up.
8.  Simply click on the *Finish*button.
